# 📘 PRODUCT REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT

## Landing Page — Web Test TKA Platform

---

# 1. 🎯 OBJECTIVE

Membuat landing page yang dapat di atur di admin dengan tujuan:

- Mengubah visitor menjadi leads (demo / kontak)
- Mengedukasi sekolah tentang value platform
- Mendorong keputusan cepat (conversion)

---

# 2. 👥 TARGET USER

- Kepala Sekolah
- Guru
- Operator sekolah
- Yayasan pendidikan

---

# 3. 🎯 PRIMARY GOALS

- Klik tombol: "Coba Demo Gratis"
- Klik tombol: "Jadwalkan Presentasi"
- Kontak WhatsApp / form

---

# 4. 🧠 POSITIONING

Platform ujian digital modern untuk sekolah:

- Mudah digunakan
- Berbasis data
- Hemat waktu
- Siap TKA

# 6. 📱 MOBILE-FIRST REQUIREMENTS

---

## 6.1 Layout

- Semua section stack vertikal
- CTA muncul tanpa scroll
- Gunakan card layout

---

## 6.2 Sticky CTA

- Tombol fixed bottom:
  "Coba Demo Gratis"

---

## 6.3 Typography

- Headline max 2 baris
- Font besar & readable

---

## 6.4 Button

- Tinggi min 48px
- Full width

---

## 6.5 Content Rules

- Hindari paragraf panjang
- Gunakan bullet point

---

# 7. 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM

---

## Colors

Primary: #2563EB  
Secondary: #7C3AED  
Accent: #F59E0B

---

## Background

- Soft gradient
- Clean white / light gray

---

## UI Style

- Rounded (16px+)
- Soft shadow
- Card-based layout

---

# 8. ⚡ ANIMATION (FRAMER MOTION)

---

## Rules

- Duration: 0.2–0.3s
- Ease: ease-in-out

---

## Effects

- Fade-in on scroll
- Hover scale (1.02)
- Button press (0.97)

---

## Avoid

- Heavy animation
- Layout shift

---
